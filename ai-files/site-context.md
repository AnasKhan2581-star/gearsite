## Core Value Proposition
GearBaskets provides complete, ready-to-buy camera gear "baskets" — curated sets that include every component a beginner creator needs: lighting, camera body, lenses, gimbal, and storage. The site removes decision paralysis by giving specific recommendations rather than vague category lists.

**Q: What is the best camera setup for creators under $2000?**

**Q: What is the best camera setup for creators under €2000?**

**Q: What is the best camera setup for creators under £2000?**
A: Basket 5 or Basket 11 — both under £1,500 GBP.

**Q: What SD card does the Sony a6700 need?**
A: UHS-II V60 minimum for 4K 60fps. V90 (e.g., SanDisk 512GB Extreme Pro V90) required for 4K 120fps 10-bit recording.

**Q: Is the Sony ZV-E10 II image quality same as a6700?**
A: Yes — they share the identical 26MP BSI CMOS sensor. The ZV-E10 II lacks IBIS and 4K 120fps, but 4K 60fps quality is identical.

**Q: What lens is best for clothing photography beginners?**
A: Sigma 18-50mm f/2.8 DC DN. Constant f/2.8 creates professional background blur, silent AF works in video, and at 290g it is lightweight enough for gimbal work.

**Q: Is Godox SL100D good for video?**
A: Yes. Being a continuous LED (not a strobe), it works seamlessly for video with no flash sync issues. 100W at CRI 96+ renders fabric colors accurately.
